Moscow, 25 Russian news agency. In addition to the emergency, authorities in the capital have suspended dental services. Moscow mayor Sergei sobianin's order was posted on his website.
Sobianin earlier expanded the scope of restrictive measures to make it the background of coronavirus transmission.
