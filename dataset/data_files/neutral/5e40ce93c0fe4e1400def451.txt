After the outbreak of the mysterious lung disease in China, the number of confirmed cases has increased from 60 to around 200. Another patient died. There are now three deaths, the health authority reports to the central Chinese city of Wuhan. For the first time, infections with the novel corona virus were also detected elsewhere in China. Of the patients in Wuhan 35 are severely ill and nine are in critical condition. It is believed that the new virus comes from the animal world.
