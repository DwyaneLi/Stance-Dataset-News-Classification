Irkutsk, 25 January. /tass/ Several BGU students from Baikal National University in China, who had previously recorded a sudden illness caused by coronavirus, have been sent to hospital for treatment. Respiratory diseases. According to preliminary information, they have influenza A, but doctors are checking for possible diseases caused by the coronavirus. Vladimir harbayev, general doctor of Irkutsk state Clinical Hospital, told reporters Saturday.
"The president told me that several Chinese students had not come to class. It turns out these students were ill two or three days ago. During the holidays, they go home and settle down in the incubation period of coronavirus infection. Five people are currently in hospital: everyone has a seasonal flu virus. But in order to completely eliminate the coronavirus infection, we sent materials to Novosibirsk for testing, "said khabadayev, who pointed out that Russia has a high incidence of heart disease. The doctor's findings are expected Saturday night.
He explained that in the dormitory of the hospital, patients carry out quarantine activities and medical staff work there. So far, no other diseases among the students have been found.
