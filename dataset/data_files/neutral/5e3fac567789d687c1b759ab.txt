Moscow, January 22. /tass/ Russia is ready to deal with the threat of the spread of the new Colorado virus, with its pandemic excluded. Alexander Petrov, a member of the State Duma Health Protection Commission, told reporters on Wednesday.
"From the perspective of epidemiological safety, the Russian Federation is very willing to do so," said the member of Parliament in response to the questions. In his view, the death rate caused by the common flu is much higher. "
Petrov pointed out that people were afraid that a new coronaveros would be so dangerous that it would cause an epidemic similar to that of the 18th-19th century, where tens of millions of people would die. " This is not going to happen. It's impossible in the modern health system, he is sure.
Chinese authorities informed the World Health Organization on December 31 of an unknown outbreak of pneumonia in Wuhan, central China. On January 7, Chinese experts discovered the pathogen of a disease - Colorado virus 2019 ncov. Chinese authorities confirmed 473 infections and 17 deaths. Corona veros is recorded in Wuhan, Beijing, Shanghai, Shenzhen and other places.
