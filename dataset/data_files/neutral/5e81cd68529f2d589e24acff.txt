Washington, 17 Russian news agency. U.S. Secretary of state Mike pompeio accused Iran of spreading false information about the new Colorado State.
In this case, despite the recommendation of the World Health Organization, the ancient city of Pompeii has been called "Wuhan City, China" for many times Although the number of virus deaths outside China has exceeded that in China.
"I would like to draw attention to the misleading campaign of the Iranian regime on the origin of the Wuhan virus. Instead of paying attention to the needs of the Iranian people and receiving sincere assistance, the Iranian leaders lied to Wuhan virus in a few weeks. " -Pompeii said at a state Department press conference.
In this way, he said, Iran's leaders are trying to evade their responsibility for "the government's actions to control the virus".
Pompeii city reiterated that the United States is ready to help Iran fight the Colorado virus and allow the purchase of medical supplies for Iran, which is not in violation of many U.S. sanctions.
Earlier, Hossein Salami, commander of the Islamic Revolutionary Guard, claimed that the new type of coronavirus could be a biological weapon of the United StatesAbout
Since the Iranian authorities first reported Colorado virus on February 19, more than 610 people have been infected with the virus, 988 of whom have died. More than 5300 have recovered. The victims of coronavirus include some senior officials and members of Parliament.
The World Health Organization announced the outbreak of a new coronavirus (covid-19) epidemic on March 11. More than 173000 people have been infected in more than 150 countries around the world, most have recovered and more than 7000 have died.
