Beijing, February 5. /tass/ Russia's first humanitarian plane arrived in China's Wuhan City on Wednesday morning, where pneumonia caused by the 2019 ncov virus broke out. This was reported on the Sina information portal.
According to his information, five Russian medical experts arrived with the goods, and they will work with Chinese medical experts to develop the Colorado virus vaccine.
At the end of December 2019, Chinese authorities recorded the outbreak of 2019 ncov pneumonia virus in Wuhan. In addition to China, the disease was found in 24 countries, including Russia. The World Health Organization recognizes an international emergency in Colorado, known as an epidemic with many hot spots. According to the latest data, there are more than 24300 cases of pneumonia caused by 2019 ncov virus in China. It is estimated that 10 million people died, 491 residents died and 892 people recovered.
