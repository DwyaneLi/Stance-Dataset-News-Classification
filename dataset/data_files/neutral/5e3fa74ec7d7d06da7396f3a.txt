Rome, 3 February - Russian news agency, Alexander rogunov. The first evacuation flight of Chinese tourists to Italy landed at Rome Airport "Fiumicino", airport news agency reported.
Angelo Borrelli, director of Italy's national civil defense Bureau, announced the organization of the air bridge on Sunday, Appointed by the executive Commissioner for state of emergency. The Council of Ministers announced the plan on Friday because it is a new type of coronavirus.
A passenger plane from Taipei arrived in Rome at 6:00. This is the first of five flights planned by China Airlines. In general, nearly 3000 Chinese citizens are planned to evacuate to China.
Later Monday, a flight carrying nearly 60 Italians hoping to return from Wuhan will land at pratika de mare military base.
On Thursday, Italian authorities confirmed that two Chinese tourists in Rome had developed a new type of coronavirus. In this regard, the CAAC announced that it would stop air services with China in addition to cargo flights. Connecting flights from China, including those from the Schengen region, were also monitored, Borelli said.
A pair of Chinese elderly tourists visited the Spallanzani Institute of infectious diseases, and their health condition was satisfactory.
