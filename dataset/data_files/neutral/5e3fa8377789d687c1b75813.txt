Haikou / China / February 1. /tass/ On Saturday, the South China Sea provincial government delivered the first batch of humanitarian materials to central China's Hubei Province, The most serious pneumonia outbreak in the country was caused by the 2019 ncov virus. This is reported by Nanhai Bay information portal.
According to his data, 109 tons of tropical fruits and fresh vegetables from Sanya will be delivered to the destination in two days. Before that, Hainan organized a professional medical team to go to Hubei to help colleagues in other regions fight against Colorado virus. Overall, the authorities mobilized about 60000 people In order to control the outbreak of pneumonia, doctors and health workers began to spread the virus.
