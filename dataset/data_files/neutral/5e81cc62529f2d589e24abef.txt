Moscow, 18 Russian news agency. Prime Minister Mikhail mishuddin has ordered temporary restrictions on flights to the UK, the US and the UAE, the government's website said.
From midnight on March 20, restrictions were imposed on the transportation of passengers from and to Russia. In addition to regular flights from Moscow's sheremejevo airport to London, New York, Abu Dhabi and between.
In addition, charter flights to export Russians from these countries will be exceptional.
The Ministry of transport should report the results to the government by March 20. The task of the Russian aviation authority is to inform airlines and other stakeholders of the restrictions within 24 hours.
Previously, Russia restricted air links with China, Iran, South Korea and EU countries, including Italy, Germany, France, Spain, Norway and Switzerland. Other countries have imposed corresponding restrictions.

According to the latest data, more than 190000 people are infected in more than 150 countries around the world. Nearly 8000 people died.
