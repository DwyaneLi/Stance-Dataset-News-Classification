Moscow, 24 Russian news agency. Moscow's Ministry of health has denied the death of a female patient in the commune because of coronaverus.
It is reported that the 69 year old patient was admitted due to contact with his daughter, who recently returned from an epidemic country. Covid-19 was negative.
Earlier, a television channel reported that a patient died in Moscow and was taken to a community hospital on March 19, suspected to be a new type of coronavirus.
The covid-19 epidemic, which first emerged on New Year's Eve in China, affected 168 countries. The virus has penetrated all continents except Antarctica. According to the World Health Organization, there are about 330000 people infected and 14500 died worldwide.
According to the statistics of Johns Hopkins University, there are more than 380000 people suffering from covid-19 disease, 16568 people died and 102000 people were treated. Italy, which has the highest death toll, has 6000, followed by China, Spain, Iran, France and the United States.
In Russia, the total number of patients with covid-19 reached 438, 262 of whom were treated in the capital and 17 recovered.
