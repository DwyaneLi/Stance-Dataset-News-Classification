Moscow, 20 March / Sputnik radio. Another 4 patients diagnosed with coronavirus recovered in Russia, Moscow reported Thursday.
As a result, the total number of people recovered reached 9.
"Nine have recovered," confirmed the news on the stokoronavirus website.
Chinese authorities informed the World Health Organization on December 31, 2019 that an unknown outbreak of pneumonia occurred in Wuhan City (Hubei Province) in Central China. Experts found that the new coronavirus had become the pathogen of the disease, which was later officially known as covid-19.
Sputnik previously reported less than 300 cases of covid-19 outside Hubei Province in China.
