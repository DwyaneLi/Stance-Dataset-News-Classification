The Japanese company Toyota announced this Wednesday that it will prolong production in its three factories in China until February 9 following the coronavirus crisis, which has already caused more than 130 deaths.
"Let's monitor the situation and make a new decision" after this date,"a group spokesperson said to the news agency France Presse.
The three Toyota factories in China had been shut down last week due to Chinese New Year holidays and festivities.
The Chinese health authorities announced five.974 confirmed cases of contamination in mainland China plus 1.400 on Tuesday, and raised the number of deaths to 132.
In Germany, the health authorities confirmed three more cases of contagion by the new coronavirus (2019-nCoV) detected in China, increasing to four the number of infected in that country. Germany is the second country in Europe affected by the outbreak after France.
In addition to mainland China, France and Germany, cases of infection have also been reported in Macao, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Thailand, Japan, South Korea, the United States, Singapore, Vietnam, Nepal, Malaysia, Australia and Canada.
Symptoms associated with coronavirus infection with the provisional name 2019-nCoV ar e more intense than influenza and include fever, pain, general malaise and respiratory distress, such as shortness of breath.
